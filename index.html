Company Profile

泉州恒远德纸制品有限公司是一家专业从事生活用纸生产与加工的现代化企业，深耕纸制品行业超过十年，产品涵盖厨房纸、卷筒卫生纸、抽纸、餐巾纸等。

Quanzhou Hengyuande Paper Products Co., Ltd. is a modern manufacturer specializing in household paper production for over ten years, offering kitchen rolls, toilet paper, facial tissues, napkins and more.
